1. Overview

You could get between £21.80 and £139.75 a week if you’re 16 to 64 and have a disability or long-term condition. How much you get depends on how your condition affects you, not what condition you have.

The money is tax-free and you can get it whether you're working or not.

If you currently get Disability Living Allowance (DLA) you'll be told when to apply for PIP - only apply when you get a letter asking you to.

If you’re not on DLA and are over 64, check if you’re eligible for Attendance Allowance

If you’re under 16, check if you’re eligible for Disability Living Allowance for children
