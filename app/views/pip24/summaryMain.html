What we’ll ask you

We need to know how your condition affects you, so you’ll be asked about the following:

About you
  • help with your application
  • nationality and where you live
  • payments from abroad
You and your condition
  • your conditions and disabilities
  • your medication
  • your treatments and therapy
  • side effects
  • changes in your condition
  • monitoring your condition
  • professionals who know about your condition
  • information you can send
  • any aids and adaptations you have
  • seeing, speaking and hearing
You and your home
  • moving around your home
  • using the toilet
  • washing yourself
  • getting dressed
  • making a hot meal from fresh ingredients
  • eating and drinking
Getting out and about
  • getting out and walking about
  • mixing with people
Making sense of things
  • planning a local journey
  • getting somewhere you have never been
  • getting somewhere you know
  • understanding written information
  • dealing with money

You'll have chance to check and change your answers at the end.

Include as much information as you can in your answers.
