2. Eligibility

To get Personal Independence Payment, you must:

  • be aged 16 to 64
  • have been a resident of England, Scotland or Wales for at least 2 of the last 3 years
  • live in England, Scotland or Wales, or live abroad as a member of the armed forces

You might still get PIP if you’re living in another EEA country or Switzerland or subject to immigration control

Your health condition or disability doesn't have to be permanent but must be expected to last for at least 12 months in total. You’ll be asked if it affects you:

  • taking your medicine or treatments
  • seeing, speaking or hearing
  • using the toilet
  • washing or bathing
  • dressing yourself
  • preparing or cooking a meal
  • eating or drinking
  • getting out and about
  • mixing with other people
  • planning or following a route
  • understanding words, sentences or signs
  • managing your money
