How you're assessed

Your claim will be assessed by a medical professional using the ‘PIP assessment criteria’. You’ll get a letter within 4 weeks with either a decision or an invitation to meet face-to-face.

Face-to-face meeting

You’ll be asked more about how your condition affects you. It can last around one hour and you can take someone with you.

You’ll get a decision letter within 3 weeks of the meeting.

How you’re paid

If you’re awarded PIP, you’ll get the money in your account within a week of getting the letter.

You’ll then get a regular payment, every 4 weeks, for up to 10 years - the end date will be in your letter.

If you disagree with your decision

Check your decision letter to see how you can ask for your decision to be reconsidered.
