5. If your circumstances change

You must call if:

  • your personal details change, eg your name, address or doctor
  • your condition or the help you need changes
  • you stay overnight in a hospital or care home
  • you go abroad for more than 13 weeks
  • you’re imprisoned or held in detention

You could have to go to court or pay a penalty if you deliberately give false or incomplete information, or do not report changes.

Your Personal Independence Payment may also be stopped or reduced.
