4. Apply

Before you apply, check you’re eligible and make sure you have:

  • your National Insurance number
  • contact details of any nurses, counsellors, doctors or other people who help you
  • details of your medication or treatments
  • your bank or building society details

If you're terminally ill call 0800 917 2222 to have your claim dealt with faster.

You’ll be asked, in detail, about the effects of your condition. You can ask a friend, family member or support organisation to help you with the application.

You can save and return to your application as often as you need in the next 28 days.
