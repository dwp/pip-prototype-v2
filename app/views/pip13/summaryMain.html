What you’ll be asked

We need to know how your condition affects you, so you’ll be asked about:

You

  • help with your application
  • nationality and where you live
  • payments from abroad

You and your condition

  • your conditions and disabilities
  • your medication
  • your treatments and therapy
  • side effects
  • changes in your condition
  • monitoring your condition
  • professionals who know about your condition
  • medical information
  • any aids and adaptations you have
  • seeing, speaking and hearing

You and your home

  • getting around your home
  • using the toilet
  • washing yourself
  • getting dressed
  • preparing and cooking food
  • eating and drinking

Getting out and about

  • getting out and about
  • mixing with people

Making sense of things

  • planning a local journey
  • going somewhere you have never been
  • going somewhere you know
  • your understanding of words, sentences, instructions and signs
  • making decisions about money

Include as much information as you can in your answers.
